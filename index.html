FAQ

Common questions about VTCFaRM

What is a container plant factory?

A fully controlled indoor growing environment inside a standard 40ft HC ISO container. Includes LED grow lights, hydroponic irrigation (NFT, DWC, or Flow & Drain), climate control, and automation. Supports cannabis, leafy greens, herbs, microgreens, and more.

How much does a container farm cost?

Leafy greens configurations start from ~$60,000 USD. Cannabis configurations from ~$75,000 USD. Pricing depends on crop type, automation level, and LED specification. Contact us for a factory-direct quote within 24 hours.

What hydroponic systems does VTCFaRM use?

NFT (Nutrient Film Technique), DWC (Deep Water Culture), and Flow & Drain (ebb and flow) — configured by crop type. We do not offer aeroponic systems. The standard leafy greens container uses a 5-tier Flow & Drain system.

What container size is standard?

Standard is 40ft High Cube (HC) ISO. 20ft is available when minimum order exceeds 10 units. Every container is fully customized to your crop, tier count, and production requirements.

Does VTCFaRM ship internationally?

Yes — factory direct to 120+ countries. We handle export documentation, logistics coordination, and delivery support. Built to ISO standards for safe international transport.

What crops can I grow?

Leafy greens (lettuce, spinach), herbs (basil, coriander, mint), microgreens, cannabis and medical plants, strawberries, tomatoes, and peppers. Each system is configured to your specific crop requirements.
