05
5-Tier High-Density System
Five stacked growing tiers utilize the full interior height of the container. Dramatically higher plant density per square metre of container footprint vs single or 2-tier systems.
06
Flow & Drain (Ebb & Flow) Technique
Nutrient solution floods each tray on a timed cycle then drains back to the reservoir. Simple, reliable, and highly effective for leafy greens and herbs. Minimal clogging risk.
07
All Leafy Green Varieties
Lettuce, salad leaves, spinach, kale, Swiss chard, rocket, pak choi, microgreens, coriander, basil, mint, parsley — any short-stature crop with 20–35 day cycle.

Container Size Policy
The standard VTCFaRM container is 40ft High Cube ONLY. The 20ft container is available exclusively when the minimum order quantity (MOQ) exceeds 10 units. This applies to all container types. VTCFaRM does not supply aeroponic irrigation systems — available options are NFT, DWC, and Flow & Drain.
